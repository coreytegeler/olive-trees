The Yazidi community in Bashiqa gather every year in April to celebrate the Yazidi New Year "Sari Sal". Despite the fact of the destruction of Shekh Hasan Shrine on that hill, they went to celebrate with positive energy the new year and also the rebuilding of the shrine and the to share the energy of hope, positivity, & resilience.
